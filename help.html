Help and FAQs

General Questions

We offer a wide range of vehicles for rent, including GTR, Mercedes, trucks, and more. You can browse our selection on our Cars page.
To make a reservation, visit our Car Reservation page and follow the instructions. You'll need to provide your contact information and desired rental dates.
We accept major credit cards, including Visa, MasterCard, and Jazzcash, Easypaisa and Cash on Delivery (COD). Payment is processed securely through our website.
You can cancel your reservation by logging into your account and navigating to the My Reservations page. Follow the cancellation instructions provided.
Basic insurance coverage is included in the rental price. Additional coverage options are available at an extra cost.
